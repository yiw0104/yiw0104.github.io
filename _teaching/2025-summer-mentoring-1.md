I served as a Postdoctoral Mentor for [the 2025 Center for the Environment Summer Undergraduate Research Program](https://environment.washu.edu/2026-summer-undergraduate-researchers/), where I supervised undergraduate student Ellie (Eliana) Hockman on a spatial mapping project examining the climate resilience of Brazil’s Minha Casa Minha Vida (MCMV) affordable housing program. I trained Ellie in conducting GIS analysis using Python, and together we geocoded 3,954 MCMV Faixa 1 housing projects funded between 2009 and 2019 across Brazil. The resulting geocoded dataset is publicly available on Harvard Dataverse and can be accessed [here](https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/B3HNWS).
